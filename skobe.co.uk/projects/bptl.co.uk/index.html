Welcome to

Brightlink Power Testing Limited

Brightlink Power Testing Limited are a substation support contractor in order to installation, testing and commissioning of transformers and switchgear substations UK wide, off-shore and overseas. With almost 25 years’ experience in the industry we have gathered the technical expertise essential for delivering quality service to our customers.

Transformers

Installation
We offer a complete installation service or alternatively supply a supervisor to oversee and guide the installation, testing and commissioning of your equipment on your behalf.
You enjoy complete peace of mind knowing we take care of every aspect of installing your transformer, from the planning, method statements and risk assessments, to liaising with the necessary authorities and booking any additional contractors or equipment required.

Commissioning
We provide highly qualified and competent commissioning engineers, who undertake the complete electrical testing and commissioning of transformers and ancillary equipment.

  • SFRA (Sweep Frequency Response Analysis)
  • Insulation Resistance
  • Capacitance and Power Factor for the Bushings and Windings
  • Voltage Ratio and Vector Check
  • Winding Resistance
  • Calibration of Oil and Winding Temperature Indicators
  • Fan, Pump and Tap changer Drive Motor Characteristics
  • Operation checks of all ancillary devices (Buchholz Relays, Pressure Relief Devices, etc.)
  • CT Class testing incl. Ratio, Resistance, Polarity, Magnetisation Curves
  • Polarisation Spectrum Analysis (Dielectric Frequency Response)
  • Oil sample tests (dielectric strength, water content, acidity, furans analysis, and dissolved gas analysis)

Switchgear

We offer a complete installation service or alternatively the supply of a supervisor to “oversee” and guide the installation, testing and commissioning of your equipment

Installation services for medium voltage switchgear products
Full installation services include:

  • Decommissioning and removal of old switchgear.
  • Site supervision
  • Installation and erection of new equipment
  • Functional testing and site handover

Commissioning of medium voltage switchgear products
Certified technicians perform commissioning of switchgear, protection and control systems as part of our equipment supply, which includes:

  • Cold commissioning to verify protection and control operation.
  • Hot commissioning and energisation of the equipment.
  • Supervision during plant start up.

Protection and control relay testing / commissioning services
Medium voltage Service has the expertise to test and commission relays and relay systems at a customer’s location to ensure that the relays are properly operating and wired correctly. Specific job scopes are developed based on customer requirements. Our engineers and technicians verify that the relay settings are correct and perform system functional tests to verify the integrity of the system. The relays are run through trip checks to make sure they pass acceptance testing. In-service tests can also be performed. System wiring is verified against as built drawings. Documentation on findings, including test reports, is provided.

Protection Panels

We can provide a complete installation and commissioning service and support to ensure that the protection schemes, disconnectors, battery chargers and all its products are implemented perfectly into the customer’s system.

Experienced engineers can perform cold and hot commissioning tests and tasks which will ensure that the products perform exactly as per their design intent. Full test documentation will be provided using either our standard testing forms or the customer’s standard forms. This service can be extended to provide ongoing maintenance where we can periodically perform routine functional and performance tests to confirm that the system is still working correctly.

Maintenance Protective relays and devices must be tested and functionality proven at certain intervals during the life of the equipment. However, when planning or carrying out primary plant maintenance the protection element is easily overlooked.

Our commissioning engineers are fully conversant with all types of modern switchgear and protection equipment and have a good knowledge of legacy equipment that is critically important when working on brownfield sites. We are equipped with the latest test equipment including 3 phase programmable relay tests sets and are equipped with the necessary software to permit the test of the most complex devices.
