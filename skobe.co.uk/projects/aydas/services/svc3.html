Testing

Primary Testing of Power Transformers

The power transformers shall be tested on site after completion of the installation and vacuum filling to ensure the electrical and mechanical condition of the transformer active part, ancillary devices, and insulating oil prior to energization.

  • Insulation Resistance
  • Winding Resistance
  • Voltage Ratio and Vector Check
  • Calibration of Winding Temperature Indicators
  • Calibration of Oil Temperature Indicators
  • Fan, Pump and Tap changer Drive Motor Characteristics
  • Operation checks of all ancillary devices (Buchholz Relays, Pressure Relief Devices, etc)
  • CT – Ratio, Resistance Polarity, Magnetisation Curves, and Test Loop Polarity
  • Tangent Delta
  • SFRA (Sweep Frequency Response Analysis)
  • Capacitance and Power Factor
  • Polarisation Spectrum Analysis (Dielectric Frequency Response)
  • Oil sample tests (dielectric strength, water content, acidity, colour, odour, visible contaminants, and dissolved gas analysis)

Test Equipment

  • OMICRON CPC 100 + CP TD1 – Multifunctional Primary Test System (Common Electrical Tests, Static & Dynamic DC Winding Resistance, Tap Changer, Short-circuit impedance / leakage reactance measurement, Excitation current), Capacitance & Power Factor of the windings and bushings.
  • OMICRON TESTRANO 600 – Transformer turns ratio, , DC winding resistance, Dynamic resistance (DRM), Short-circuit impedance / leakage reactance, Frequency response of stray losses (FRSL), Demagnetization. Exciting current, Power/dissipation factor and capacitance (with CP TD1 accessory).
  • OMICRON CT Analyser – Protection and metering current transformers (Ratio and phase angle, CT excitation and saturation, burden impedance, winding resistance, CT Polarity)
  • OMICRON FRANEO – Sweep Frequency Response Analysis (SFRA)
  • OMICRON DIRANA – Dielectric Frequency Response (DFR) – ‘Polarisation Spectrum Measurement
  • Buchholz Dry Air Pump – Buchholz relay function test
  • Hart Scientific 7103 Temperature Bath – WI temperature sensors calibration
  • MIT525 5KV Megger – Insulation resistance test
