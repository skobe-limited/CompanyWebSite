Transformer Failure Analysis

Our experts can perform systemically investigation in pursuit of carrying out on-site “online and offline” tests to determine the root cause of the failure and prevent it. These studies are carried out independently and in accordance with the customer’s privacy principles.

The collection of all available data ensures that the investigation is concluded in an effective and rapid manner. Things to do when a failure occurs:

  • Call us as soon as possible
  • Do not scrap any part of the transformer
  • Collect information about the transformer failure as much as possible (protection recording, photos, etc.)
  • Provide copy of the transformer’s factory acceptance tests and, if possible, design data.
  • Collect maintenance records and any results of transformer tests on site (commissioning, DGAs, etc.)
