Commissioning

Aydas Transformer Services provides comprehensive commissioning service to all types of large power transformers and shunt reactors, meeting the exacting standards of all Transmission and Distribution Network Operators.

Commissioning activities can be group under to category as prior to energizing and after energizing.

Prior to energizing the following items should be reviewed:

  • Visual inspection on the transformer/shunt reactor
  • Checking of impact recorder
  • Checking of painting, leakages and fastening
  • Belding/ air ventilation on bushings’ radiators, conservator etc.
  • Earthing
  • Cooling equipment
  • Tap changer, oil filter and motor drive unit
  • Current transformers
  • Protective Equipment
  • Oil sample
  • Electrical tests

Documentation

  • Automated Test Reports from specialised test and commissioning equipment
  • Customised and system specific test documentation
  • Detail report on work completion with test results and further recommendations

After Energizing

When the transformer/shunt reactor has been energized and during the first few hours which follow, continuous checks must be carried out. They include:

  • Listening for unusual noises emanating from within the transformer tank
  • Listening to the fans and pumps for unusual noises
  • Checking for any signs of an oil leak
  • Checking the oil levels in the various compartments remains correct
  • Checking of the oil and winding temperatures
  • Checking that the breather work properly (bubbles in the glass)
